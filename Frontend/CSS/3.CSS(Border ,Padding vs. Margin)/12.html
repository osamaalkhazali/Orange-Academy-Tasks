Fox

Foxes are small to medium-sized, omnivorous mammals belonging to several genera of the family Canidae. They have a flattened skull, upright triangular ears, a pointed, slightly upturned snout, and a long bushy tail.

Foxes are known for their cunning nature and adaptability. They can be found in various habitats, including forests, grasslands, mountains, and deserts. They are also known to thrive in urban areas, where they scavenge for food.
