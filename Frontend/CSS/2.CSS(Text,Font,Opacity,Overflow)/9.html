Amblyopia (Lazy Eye)

What is Lazy Eye?

Lazy eye, or amblyopia, is a vision development disorder where an eye fails to achieve normal visual acuity, even with prescription eyeglasses or contact lenses. It typically begins during infancy and early childhood. In most cases, only one eye is affected, but in some cases, reduced visual acuity can occur in both eyes.
